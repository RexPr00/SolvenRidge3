What drives returns

Returns usually move with three practical drivers.

Meaning: demand strength is the main return engine.

Investor gain: focus on the factor with most impact.

Simple interpretation

  • Demand growth: more tenants seek quality units.
  • Pricing gap: underpriced assets can rerate.
  • Renovations: small upgrades can raise rent.

What could go wrong: weak demand can reduce both rent and exits.
